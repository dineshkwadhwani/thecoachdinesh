My Journey

My leadership journey began as a software engineer passionate about building technology products. Over the past two decades, I have grown through multiple leadership roles in global organisations, leading distributed engineering teams and delivering enterprise platforms used by millions.


As I moved into senior leadership roles, I discovered that leadership success was not just about technical capability. It required clarity of thinking, the ability to influence stakeholders, and the ability to develop other leaders.
